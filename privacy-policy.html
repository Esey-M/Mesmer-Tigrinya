🔒 ምሕላው ውሽጢ / Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

🛡️ ኣገዳሲ ሓበሬታ / Important Information

We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of children using our educational platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

📊 እንታይ ሓበሬታ ንሰብስብ / What Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name and email address when you contact us
  • Usage Data: Quiz scores, progress, and learning preferences (stored locally in your browser)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and website usage statistics

🎯 እንታይ ንጥቀመሉ / How We Use Your Information

  • Educational Purpose: To provide personalized learning experiences and track progress
  • Communication: To respond to your questions and provide support
  • Improvement: To enhance our educational content and user experience
  • Analytics: To understand how our platform is used and make improvements

👶 ንህጻናት ምሕላው / Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Our platform is designed to be safe for children. All learning data is stored locally in the browser and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly contact us.

🍪 ኩኪስ / Cookies and Local Storage

We use local browser storage to:

  • Remember your quiz progress and scores
  • Save your sound preferences
  • Maintain your learning session

This data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties.

🔐 ምሕላው ሓበሬታ / Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Learning data is stored locally in your browser
  • Contact form submissions are handled securely through Formsubmit
  • We do not store personal information on our servers

📧 ኣብ ርክብ ንኺድ / Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Through our contact form
  • We will respond within 24 hours

🔄 ምሕዳስ / Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
