💡 Learning Tips

  • Start with the alphabet book to build a strong foundation
  • Practice daily for 15-20 minutes
  • Use the books alongside our interactive quizzes
  • Encourage your child to read aloud
  • Make learning fun with games and activities

🎯 Learning Goals

  • Recognize all 33 Tigrinya letters
  • Learn basic vocabulary (100+ words)
  • Understand simple sentence structure
  • Build confidence in reading Tigrinya
  • Connect with cultural heritage
